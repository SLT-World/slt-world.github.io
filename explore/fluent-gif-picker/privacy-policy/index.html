Information We Collect

Fluent GIF Picker itself does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

However, Fluent GIF Picker retrieves GIF content from third-party services based on your searches.

When you use the search feature, your query may be sent to the selected GIF provider.

settings

Third-Party Services

Fluent GIF Picker uses external GIF providers (such as KLIPY, Tenor, GIPHY, or others depending on your selection).

When you search for GIFs:

  1. Your search terms are sent to the selected provider.
  2. The provider may collect data according to their own privacy policies.
  3. Fluent GIF Picker does not control or store this data.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of the providers they choose to use.

Data Transmission

Fluent GIF Picker:

  1. Does not upload personal files to its own servers.
  2. Does not include analytics, tracking, or advertising systems.

However:

  1. Network requests are made to third-party GIF providers to retrieve content.
  2. These requests are necessary for the app to function.

Local Data Storage

Fluent GIF Picker may store certain preferences locally on your device, such as:

  1. Selected GIF provider.
  2. App settings (e.g., quality, filters, preferences).
  3. Recently used GIFs.

This data is:

  1. Stored only on your device.
  2. Not transmitted to any external servers by the app.

Fluent GIF Picker includes optional content filtering settings to help control visible results, but cannot guarantee that all content will be filtered.
