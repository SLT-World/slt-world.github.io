Information We Collect

Recodite does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Recodite operates entirely on your device. All processing such as media compression and file handling is performed locally on your computer.

No files are uploaded to the cloud, no analytics or tracking is used, and no information is shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

All files you select in Recodite remain on your device.

Recodite:

  1. Does not upload or transmit files to external servers.
  2. Does not access files outside of what you explicitly select.

Third-Party Services

Recodite does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or telemetry services.

Any local third-party libraries used by Recodite for processing media operate entirely offline and do not communicate with external services.
